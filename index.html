Touch. Push. You're home.

A door that works for you. Not against you. No keys, no apps, no screens. Touch your ring and push. Deliveries land inside. Everyone gets in, their way.

Every line of code is open source. No cloud. No subscription. Your door, your data, your rules.

How it works

  • NFC unlock
    Touch your ring to the handle and push. One motion, you're in.
  • Delivery flap
    Parcels go through the door into a bag inside your home. Safe, dry, yours.
  • Capacitive exit
    Touch the interior handle to leave. No button, no twist, no thought.
  • Knock pattern
    Three knocks, pause, two knocks. The kids learned it like a game.
  • QR code
    Grandma shows a photo to the door. It opens. She didn't install anything.
  • Phone wallet
    Apple Wallet, Google Wallet, Samsung Wallet. Coming via Aliro.

Made to order

Every door is built to fit. We specialise in properties where off-the-shelf doesn't work — listed buildings, heritage homes, non-standard openings. Priced competitively with the bespoke doors you'd buy anyway, except ours actually do something.

Open source

Firmware, PCB designs, mechanical drawings — all published. You can audit the code that controls your front door. Security and privacy shouldn't require trust in a corporation.
